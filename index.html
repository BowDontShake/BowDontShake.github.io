About

This site is a public health collaboration originally created by David Booth, PhD, and Rafael Richards, MD MS, but now representing the contributions of many individuals and organizations. The practice of shaking hands -- especially between healthcare workers and patients -- adds a recklessly unnecessary vector for disease transmission. With your help we can change this cultural practice to a safer, healthier alternative: a brief, gentle bow.

For press inquiries and technical questions, contact david@dbooth.org .
