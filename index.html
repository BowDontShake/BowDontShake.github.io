BOW, DON'T SHAKE!

BE SAFE. BE RESPONSIBLE.
REDUCE DISEASE TRANSMISSION.
#BowDontShake

The practice of shaking hands -- especially between healthcare workers and patients -- adds a needless and dangerous vector for disease transmission. With your help we can change this cultural practice to a safer, healthier alternative that is understood throughout the world: a brief, gentle bow.
